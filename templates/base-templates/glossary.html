Glossary of Statistics

Statistic Abbreviation Statistic Name Definition
ERA+ Adjusted Earned Run Average A normalized version of ERA that accounts for external factors like ballparks and opponents
A Assists Awarded to a defensive player who touches the ball prior to a runner being thrown out
AB At Bats A plate appearance that ends with a batter being out (except sacrifice plays) or ends with a player reaching a base by a hit, error, or fielder's choice
AB/HR At Bats per Home Run The number of at bats a player has divided by the number of home runs a player has hit
AB/RBI At Bats per Run Batted In The number of at bats a player has divided by the number RBIs that a player has
AB/SO At Bats per Strikeout The number of at bats a player has divided by the number times a player has struck out
aLI Average Leverage Index The average pressure a batter or pitcher saw during the season
BIP% Balls In Play Percentage The percentage of at bats that have resulted in a player putting a ball in play
BB/9 Base on Balls per 9 Innings The number of bases on balls allowed by a pitcher for every 9 innings
BB Bases on Balls/Walks A batter receives four balls in a singular plate appearance and is awarded first base
BB% Bases On Balls Percentage The percentage of at bats that have resulted in a player receiving a strike out
BAA Batted Average Against The number of hits allowed by a pitcher divided by the number of at-bats
BAbipA Batted Average on Balls in Play Against The batting average allowed by a pitcher for balls that are put in play (excluding home runs, strikeouts, and sacrifice flies)
BF Batters Faced The number of plate appearances a pitcher has faced
BA Batting Average The total number of hits by a player divided by the total number of at-bats
BAbip Batting Average on Balls In Play A player's batting average for balls that are put in play (excluding home runs, strikeouts, and sacrifice flies)
CS Caught Stealing A base runner is thrown out in between pitches
CG Complete Games A pitcher has thrown to every batter in a single game regardless of the length of a game
DC Defensive Chances The number of opportunities that a player has to record an out
dWAR Defensive Wins Above Replacement A measure of how valuable a player is on defense over another who could replace them at the same position in terms of wins
N/A Division Rank A team's rank in their division at the end of the season
2B Doubles A batter puts the ball in play and reaches second base without the help of an error or fielder's choice play
ERA Earned Run Average The average number of earned runs for every 9 innings
ER Earned Runs The total number of runs given up by a pitcher without the assist of an error or passed ball
E Errors A defensive player fails to successfully make a play that an average fielder should be able to make
XBH% Extra Base Hit Percentage The percentage of at bats that have resulted in a player hitting and reaching second base, third base, and home plate
XBHA% Extra Base Hit Percentage The percentage of at-bats that a pitcher has allowed a player to get a hit that results in the batter reaching second base, third base, and home plate
FIP Fielding Independent Pitching Measures the plays that the pitcher has the most control over (strikeouts, unintentional walks, hit-by-pitches, and home runs)
Fld% Fielding Percentage The number of putouts and assists a defensive player has divided by the total number of defensive chances a player has
GF Games Finished A relief pitcher who threw the final pitch of the game for their team
GP Games played The number of games that a player has entered a game at any point
GS Games Started The number of games that a player has thrown the first pitch for their team
H Hits A batter puts the ball in play and reaches at least first base safely without the help of an error or fielder's choice play
HA Hits allowed The total number of hits given up by a pitcher
H/9 Hits per 9 innings The number of hits allowed by a pitcher for every 9 innings
HR% Home Run Percentage The percentage of at bats that have resulted in a player hitting a home run
HR Home Runs A batter puts a ball in play and reaches home plate safely without the help of an error or fielder's choice play
HRA Home Runs Allowed The total number of home runs given up by a pitcher
HR/9 Home Runs per 9 Innings The number of home runs allowed by a pitcher for every 9 innings
IP Innings played The number of innings that a pitcher has thrown in thirds (each out is a third of an inning)
ISO Isolated Power An indicator of how often a player hits for extra bases
LD% Line Drive Percentage The percentage of balls put into play that are line drives
oRAR Offensive Runs Above Replacement A measure of how many runs a player contributes offensively compared to another who could replace them at the same position
oWAR Offensive Wins Above Replacement A measure of how valuable a player is on offense over another who could replace them at the same position in terms of wins
OBP On-Base Percentage The total number of hits, walks, and hit-by-pitches divided by the total number of plate appearances
OBPA On-Base Percentage Against The total number of hits, walks, and hit-by-pitches allowed by a pitcher divided by the total number of plate appearances
OPS On-base Percentage Plus Slugging Percentage A number that indicates how well a player can safely reach base and hit for power
OPSA On-base Percentage Plus Slugging Percentage Against A number that indicates how well players safely reach base and hit for power against a pitcher
OPS+ On-base Percentage Plus Slugging Percentage Plus A number used to normalize a player's On Base Percentage across all batters in the league
O Outs Recorded when an offensive player is retired by the defense
N/A Payroll The amount of money spent by a team on their roster in a season
PA Plate Appearances Each completed turn batting regardless of the outcome
rOBA Player Total Offensive Contributions A measure of a player's total offensive contributions per plate appearance
P Plays Included in Win Probability Added The number of plays that are included in the calculation of Win Probability Added
Pos Position(s) The position(s) that a player plays
PO Putouts The fielder who is holding the ball when an out is made is credited in it
RF/9 Range Factor per 9 Innings An estimate on how much field a player can cover by looking at how many plays a player can make for every 9 innings
RF/G Range Factor per game An estimate on how much field a player can cover by looking at how many plays a player can make for every 9 innings
RS% Run Scoring Percentage The percentage of times that a base runner eventually scores a run
R Runs A base runner touches home plate safely
RAA Runs Above Average A measure of how may runs a player contributes
RAR Runs Above Replacement A measure of how many runs a player contributes compared to another who could replace them at the same position
RA Runs allowed The total number of runs given up by a pitcher
RBI Runs Batted In A batter receives an RBI for each runner that reaches home plate after the ball is in play (not counted if the batter becomes out or there is an error)
RC Runs Created The offensive contribution that a player has made to their team
RC/G Runs Created per game The offensive contribution that a player has made to their team per game
SV Saves Credited to the final relief pitcher for the winning team who entered the game during a Save Opportunity
N/A Season Outcome The state of the season that a team's season has ended
SHO Shutouts A pitcher that throws an entire game and does not give up a run to the opposing team
WPA/LI Situational Wins The sum of Win Probability Added divided by the leverage index for each play
SLG Slugging Percentage A number that indicates how well a player hits for extra base hits
SLGA Slugging Percentage Against A number that indicates how well players hit for extra base hits against a pitcher
SB% Stolen Base Percentage The percentage successful stolen bases that a player has on total stolen base attempts
SB Stolen Bases A base runner successfully reaches the next base without the batter putting the ball in play and no error occurs
SO% Strikeout Percentage The percentage of at bats that have resulted in a player striking out
SO Strikeouts A batter receives three strikes in a singular plate appearance and is out
SO/9 Strikeouts per 9 Innings The number of strikeouts by a pitcher for every 9 innings
SO/W Strikeouts per Walk The number of strikeouts a player receives divided by the number of walks a player receives
TB Total Bases The total number of bases earned by a batter as a result of all of their base hits
FRAA Total Fielding Runs Above Average The number of runs above or below the average a player is worth based on the number of plays made
3B Triples A batter puts the ball in play and reaches third base without the help of an error or fielder's choice play
WHIP Walks and Hits Per Inning Pitched The total number of walks and hits allowed by a pitcher divided by the number of innings they pitched
WP Wild Pitches A pitch thrown far out of the strike zone that is not caught by a catcher and results in at least one base runner advancing
WPA+ Win Probability Added The sum of the total positive events made by a player
WPAo Win Probability Added by Offensive Player The change in win probability cause by a batter during the game
WPAp Win Probability Added by Pitcher The change in win probability cause by a pitcher during the game
WPA- Win Probability Subtracted The sum of the total negative events made by a player
W% Winning Percentage The number of wins recorded divided by the total number of games
WAR Wins Above Replacement A measure of how valuable a player is over another who could replace them at the same position in terms of wins
*These definitions were provided by Baseball Training World and Baseball Reference
