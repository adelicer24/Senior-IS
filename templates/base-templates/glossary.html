Glossary of Statistics

Statistic CategoryStatistic AbbreviationStatistic NameDefinition
Advanced PitchingERA+Adjusted Earned Run AverageA normalized version of ERA that accounts for external factors like ballparks and opponents
Standard FieldingAAssistsAwarded to a defensive player who touches the ball prior to a runner being thrown out
BattingABAt-BatsA plate appearance that ends with a batter being out (except sacrifice plays) or ends with a player reaching a base by a hit, error, or fielder's choice
Advanced BattingAB/HRAt-Bats per Home RunThe number of at bats a player has divided by the number of home runs a player has hit
Advanced BattingAB/RBIAt-Bats per Run Batted InThe number of at bats a player has divided by the number RBIs that a player has
Advanced BattingAB/SOAt-Bats per StrikeoutThe number of at bats a player has divided by the number times a player has struck out
Sabermetric Batting and Sabermetric PitchingaLIAverage Leverage IndexThe average pressure a batter or pitcher saw during the season
Advanced BattingBIP%Balls In Play PercentageThe percentage of at bats that have resulted in a player putting a ball in play
Advanced PitchingBB/9Base on Balls per 9 InningsThe number of bases on balls allowed by a pitcher for every 9 innings
Standard Batting and Standard PitchingBBBases on Balls/WalksA batter receives four balls in a singular plate appearance and is awarded first base
Advanced Batting and Advanced PitchingBB%Bases On Balls PercentageThe percentage of at bats that have resulted in a player receiving a strike out
Advanced PitchingBAABatting Average AllowedThe number of hits allowed by a pitcher divided by the number of at-bats
Sabermetric PitchingBAbipABatting Average on Balls in Play AllowedThe batting average allowed by a pitcher for balls that are put in play (excluding home runs, strikeouts, and sacrifice flies)
Standard PitchingBFBatters FacedThe number of plate appearances a pitcher has faced
Standard BattingBABatting AverageThe total number of hits by a player divided by the total number of at-bats
Sabermetric BattingBAbipBatting Average on Balls In PlayA player's batting average for balls that are put in play (excluding home runs, strikeouts, and sacrifice flies)
Standard BattingCSCaught StealingA base runner is thrown out in between pitches
Standard Fielding and PitchingCGComplete GamesA pitcher has thrown to every batter in a single game regardless of the length of a game
Advanced FieldingDCDefensive ChancesThe number of opportunities that a player has to record an out
Sabermetric BattingdWARDefensive Wins Above ReplacementA measure of how valuable a player is on defense over another who could replace them at the same position in terms of wins
TeamsN/ADivision OutcomeA team's rank in their division at the end of the season
Standard Batting2BDoublesA batter puts the ball in play and reaches second base without the help of an error or fielder's choice play
Standard PitchingERAEarned Run AverageThe average number of earned runs for every 9 innings
Standard PitchingEREarned RunsThe total number of runs given up by a pitcher without the assist of an error or passed ball
Standard FieldingEErrorsA defensive player fails to successfully make a play that an average fielder should be able to make
Advanced BattingXBH%Extra Base Hit PercentageThe percentage of at bats that have resulted in a player hitting and reaching second base, third base, and home plate
Sabermetric PitchingFIPFielding Independent PitchingMeasures the plays that the pitcher has the most control over (strikeouts, unintentional walks, hit-by-pitches, and home runs)
Advanced FieldingFld%Fielding PercentageThe number of putouts and assists a defensive player has divided by the total number of defensive chances a player has
Standard PitchingGFGames FinishedA relief pitcher who threw the final pitch of the game for their team
AllGPGames playedThe number of games that a player has entered a game at any point
Standard Fielding and PitchingGSGames StartedThe number of games that a player has thrown the first pitch for their team
Standard BattingHHitsA batter puts the ball in play and reaches at least first base safely without the help of an error or fielder's choice play
Standard PitchingHAHits allowedThe total number of hits given up by a pitcher
Advanced PitchingH/9Hits Allowed per 9 inningsThe number of hits allowed by a pitcher for every 9 innings
Advanced Batting and Advanced PitchingHR%Home Run PercentageThe percentage of at bats that have resulted in a player hitting a home run
Standard BattingHRHome RunsA batter puts a ball in play and reaches home plate safely without the help of an error or fielder's choice play
Standard PitchingHRAHome Runs AllowedThe total number of home runs given up by a pitcher
Advanced PitchingHR/9Home Runs Allowed per 9 InningsThe number of home runs allowed by a pitcher for every 9 innings
Fileding and Standard PitchingIPInnings playedThe number of innings that a pitcher has thrown in thirds (each out is a third of an inning)
Advanced BattingISOIsolated PowerAn indicator of how often a player hits for extra bases
Advanced BattingLD%Line Drive PercentageThe percentage of balls put into play that are line drives
Sabermetric BattingoRAROffensive Runs Above ReplacementA measure of how many runs a player contributes offensively compared to another who could replace them at the same position
Sabermetric BattingoWAROffensive Wins Above ReplacementA measure of how valuable a player is on offense over another who could replace them at the same position in terms of wins
Advanced BattingOBPOn-Base PercentageThe total number of hits, walks, and hit-by-pitches divided by the total number of plate appearances
Advanced PitchingOBPAOn-Base Percentage AllowedThe total number of hits, walks, and hit-by-pitches allowed by a pitcher divided by the total number of plate appearances
Advanced BattingOPSOn-base Percentage Plus Slugging PercentageA number that indicates how well a player can safely reach base and hit for power
Advanced PitchingOPSAOn-base Percentage Plus Slugging Percentage AllowedA number that indicates how well players safely reach base and hit for power against a pitcher
Sabermetric BattingOPS+On-base Percentage Plus Slugging Percentage PlusA number used to normalize a player's On Base Percentage across all batters in the league
Standard BattingOOutsRecorded when an offensive player is retired by the defense
TeamsN/APayrollThe amount of money spent by a team on their roster in a season
BattingPAPlate AppearancesEach completed turn batting regardless of the outcome
Sabermetric BattingrOBAPlayer Total Offensive ContributionsA measure of a player's total offensive contributions per plate appearance
Sabermetric Batting and Sabermetric PitchingPPlays Included in Win Probability AddedThe number of plays that are included in the calculation of Win Probability Added
Standard Batting and FieldingPosPosition(s)The position(s) that a player plays
Standard FieldingPOPutoutsThe fielder who is holding the ball when an out is made is credited in it
Advanced FieldingRF/9Range Factor per 9 InningsAn estimate on how much field a player can cover by looking at how many plays a player can make for every 9 innings
Advanced FieldingRF/GRange Factor per gameAn estimate on how much field a player can cover by looking at how many plays a player can make for every 9 innings
Advanced BattingRS%Run Scoring PercentageThe percentage of times that a base runner eventually scores a run
Standard BattingRRunsA base runner touches home plate safely
Sabermetric BattingRAARuns Above AverageA measure of how may runs a player contributes
Sabermetric Batting and Sabermetric PitchingRARRuns Above ReplacementA measure of how many runs a player contributes compared to another who could replace them at the same position
Standard PitchingRARuns allowedThe total number of runs given up by a pitcher
Standard BattingRBIRuns Batted InA batter receives an RBI for each runner that reaches home plate after the ball is in play (not counted if the batter becomes out or there is an error)
Advanced BattingRCRuns CreatedThe offensive contribution that a player has made to their team
Advanced BattingRC/GRuns Created per gameThe offensive contribution that a player has made to their team per game
Standard PitchingSVSavesCredited to the final relief pitcher for the winning team who entered the game during a Save Opportunity
TeamsN/ASeason OutcomeThe state of the season that a team's season has ended
Standard PitchingSHOShutoutsA pitcher that throws an entire game and does not give up a run to the opposing team
Sabermetric Batting and Sabermetric PitchingWPA/LISituational WinsThe sum of Win Probability Added divided by the leverage index for each play
Advanced BattingSLGSlugging PercentageA number that indicates how well a player hits for extra base hits
Advanced PitchingSLGASlugging Percentage AllowedA number that indicates how well players hit for extra base hits against a pitcher
Advanced BattingSB%Stolen Base PercentageThe percentage successful stolen bases that a player has on total stolen base attempts
Standard BattingSBStolen BasesA base runner successfully reaches the next base without the batter putting the ball in play and no error occurs
Advanced Batting and Advanced PitchingSO%Strikeout PercentageThe percentage of at bats that have resulted in a player striking out
Standard Batting and Standard PitchingSOStrikeoutsA batter receives three strikes in a singular plate appearance and is out
Advanced PitchingSO/9Strikeouts per 9 InningsThe number of strikeouts by a pitcher for every 9 innings
Advanced Batting and Advanced PitchingSO/WStrikeouts per WalkThe number of strikeouts a player receives divided by the number of walks a player receives
Standard BattingTBTotal BasesThe total number of bases earned by a batter as a result of all of their base hits
Advanced FieldingFRAATotal Fielding Runs Above AverageThe number of runs above or below the average a player is worth based on the number of plays made
Batting, Fielding, and PitchingTOTTotal statistics for the seasonIf a player has played for two or more teams in a season, the statistics in this row represents the total statistics recorded for the season
Standard Batting3BTriplesA batter puts the ball in play and reaches third base without the help of an error or fielder's choice play
Advanced PitchingWHIPWalks and Hits Per Inning PitchedThe total number of walks and hits allowed by a pitcher divided by the number of innings they pitched
Standard PitchingWPWild PitchesA pitch thrown far out of the strike zone that is not caught by a catcher and results in at least one base runner advancing
Sabermetric Batting and Sabermetric PitchingWPA+Win Probability AddedThe sum of the total positive events made by a player
Sabermetric BattingWPAoWin Probability Added by Offensive PlayerThe change in win probability cause by a batter during the game
Sabermetric PitchingWPApWin Probability Added by PitcherThe change in win probability cause by a pitcher during the game
Sabermetric Batting and Sabermetric PitchingWPA-Win Probability SubtractedThe sum of the total negative events made by a player
Standard Pitching and TeamsW%Winning PercentageThe number of wins recorded divided by the total number of games
Sabermetric Batting and Sabermetric PitchingWARWins Above ReplacementA measure of how valuable a player is over another who could replace them at the same position in terms of wins
